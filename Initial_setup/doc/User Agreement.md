# OBARA Welding Gun Selection Database User Agreement

## Article 1: Purpose of the Agreement

This Agreement aims to regulate the use of the OBARA Welding Gun Selection Database (hereinafter referred to as the "Database"), protect the security and confidentiality of technical data, and ensure the reasonable and lawful use of this Database and all its functions, including but not limited to AI search functionality, product query, and technical data download.

## Article 2: Scope of Application

This Agreement applies to all users of the Database, including but not limited to employees, partners, customers, and other authorized users of OBARA (NANJING) MACHINERY & ELECTRIC CO.,LTD.

## Article 3: User Rights and Obligations

### User Rights:
1. Query and download welding gun technical data within the authorized scope.
2. Use the Database for product selection and technical reference.
3. Access and use the AI search functionality provided by the Database to obtain intelligent product recommendations and technical support.
4. Obtain technical support and services.
5. Provide suggestions and feedback on Database functions.

### User Obligations:
1. Strictly protect usernames and passwords, and do not disclose them to third parties.
2. Use technical data only for product selection, and not for other commercial purposes.
3. Do not copy, disseminate, or provide technical data to third parties.
4. Report system problems or security risks in a timely manner.
5. Comply with the Database usage specifications and refrain from any operations that may affect the normal operation of the Database.
6. Use the AI search functionality reasonably, and do not input illegal, non-compliant, or irrelevant content.

## Article 4: Confidentiality of Technical Data

1. All technical data in the Database are technical secrets of OBARA (NANJING) MACHINERY & ELECTRIC CO.,LTD.
2. Users shall not use technical data for reverse engineering, imitation, or other infringing acts.
3. Technical data is for reference only and does not constitute any legal basis.
4. Users shall take necessary measures to protect the confidentiality of technical data obtained from the Database.

## Article 5: Disclaimer

1. OBARA (NANJING) MACHINERY & ELECTRIC CO.,LTD. does not guarantee the complete accuracy of the technical data in the Database.
2. The company shall not be liable for any losses caused by changes in technical data.
3. Users should conduct technical verification based on actual circumstances.
4. The company does not assume direct responsibility for suggestions and results provided by the AI search functionality, and users should verify their accuracy themselves.
5. The company does not guarantee the absolute continuity and availability of Database services.

## Article 6: Breach of Contract

If a user violates this Agreement, OBARA (NANJING) MACHINERY & ELECTRIC CO.,LTD. has the right to:
1. Immediately suspend the user's access rights.
2. Require the user to bear corresponding legal liabilities.
3. Reserve the right to pursue further legal liabilities.
4. Take legal measures against users who maliciously use the AI search functionality or other Database functions.

## Article 7: Amendment of Agreement

OBARA (NANJING) MACHINERY & ELECTRIC CO.,LTD. reserves the right to amend this Agreement at any time, and the amended Agreement will be published in the Database. Continued use of the Database by users after the amendment constitutes acceptance of the amended Agreement.

## Article 8: Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through friendly negotiation; if negotiation fails, the dispute shall be submitted to the Nanjing Arbitration Commission for arbitration.

## Article 9: Effectiveness of Agreement

This Agreement shall become effective upon the user's click of agreement.
